Objectives
Skills learned from R assignments will be applied in a final project done in Rstudio. Students use an existing data set in order to write annotated code to 1) import the data set into R, 2) clean the data, 3) use a graph to visualize variables of interest, 4) check for assumptions for a statistical test, and 5) perform a statistical analysis of their variables of interest.

The Biostatistics course at our institution offers a comprehensive exploration of statistical principles vital in biological and health sciences. With a specialized emphasis on the R language, students delve into the application of statistical methods within the context of biological research and health data analysis. The curriculum covers a wide array of topics, ranging from foundational statistical theories to practical hands-on sessions utilizing R programming. Through engaging lessons, students learn to manipulate data, perform statistical analyses, and visualize results using R, empowering them with essential skills in conducting rigorous and insightful statistical research within the realm of life sciences.
